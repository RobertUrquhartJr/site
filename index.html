Good Breaks

whatever God is to you, whatever it may be,
remember that He made you and I alike.
we are so very different, you say, and I agree.
but like the sun and moon, who both give off light,
in so many cases He made us alike.

9.

i can smell her perfume when the wind blows just right.
...never really been a gambling man.
she likes sunflowers, i know this because i care.
finding something to have is good, finding her was better.
at least where I sit I'm doing okay.
if she loved me enough to fill the little pocket on my jeans, that'd be enough.
i treat her good, she treats me better.
...I can live with that.
